The Picard

We are a specialised campervan conversion company who pride ourselves in delivering high quality Volkswagen T5/T6 van conversions. We have over 12 years experience in sourcing the correct base van and converting it to the perfect Campervan. Based at our dedicated workshop located in Tewkesbury, just 0.5 mile from Junction 9 of the M5. We are happy to welcome people to view our vans either fully converted or in the conversion process, or simply to discuss their requirements. For examples of our conversions please go to our Gallery for pictures and videos.
